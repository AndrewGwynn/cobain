Kurt listened to all kinds of different artists including The Ramones, Electric Light Orchestra, The Beatles and many more. Nirvana

Through attending these punk shows, Kurt met Krist Novoselic, and the two began to meet up to play music together. Eventually they began to form the beginnings of Nirvana. In the early touring years, Nirvana went through numerous drummers. Eventually they settled on Chad Channing and recorded their debut album Bleach. After becoming dissatisfied with Channng's style of drumming, the band began searching for a new drummer before settling on Dave Grohl. They then went back into the studio and found their greatest success through the 1991 album Nevermind.
